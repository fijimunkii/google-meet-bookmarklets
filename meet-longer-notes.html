Meet → Longer Notes

One-click bookmarklet that flips Gemini meeting notes to Longer via the UpdateMeetingSpace RPC.

Drag this to your bookmarks bar:

📝 Meet → Longer Notes

How to install

  1. Show bookmarks bar in Chrome: ⌘⇧B (Mac) / Ctrl+Shift+B (Win).
  2. Drag the blue button above to your bookmarks bar.
  3. While in an active Meet, click the bookmark.
  4. Toast top-right shows progress: "Waiting for Meet RPC…" → "lvl=1 200".
How it works: The bookmarklet hooks window.fetch and waits for Meet to fire any RPC to MeetingSpaceService (it does this constantly during a meeting — heartbeats, presence, layout, etc.). When it sees one, it grabs the auth headers + decodes the space ID from x-goog-meeting-identifier, then immediately fires its own UpdateMeetingSpace with smart_notes_detail_level = 1. Once captured, you can experiment from console without re-clicking.

Experimenting from the console

After clicking the bookmarklet once and seeing a 200, open DevTools console and try other enum values:

setNotesDetailLevel(1)   // Standard (confirmed)
setNotesDetailLevel(2)   // Longer (confirmed)
setNotesDetailLevel(0)   // ?
setNotesDetailLevel(3)   // ?

The captured headers are reused, so these calls fire instantly.

If something breaks

  • "No Meet RPC seen" after 15s — you're probably not in an active meeting, or Meet's heartbeat is suppressed (rare).
  • 4xx response — toast and console show the status + response body. "invalid argument" = body framing wrong; "unsafe for trusted domain" = something rejected the captured auth (shouldn't happen since we're reusing Meet's own valid header).
  • Wrong meeting — if you click the bookmarklet then switch meetings, __meetCtx is stale. Re-click in the new meeting.
